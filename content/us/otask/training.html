oTask Getting Started

How do I create a task?

【iPhone、iPad & Android】
To create a new task, click the plus icon on the bottom left after login.
Type the title of task and tap "Done" to add it to your list.

Note: New task is in your "Next" box.

What can I define to a task?

When creating a new task you can quickly add all important information.
Required fields：Title、Stage.
Optional fields：Project, Assigned to, Due date, Description, Attachment, Message.

When create a new task, project is null. It means only you can see this task. You can move it to a project to share the task.
"Assigned to" for a new task is yourself. If you want to get things done by another user, please change "Assigned to".

How do I check my tasks?

In oTask, according to the priority and status, everything comes with lists that are ready for your to-dos: Today, Next, Someday, Done.

  1. [Today], Here you can see what do you want to do today?
    To create a new "Today" task, click the plus icon on the bottom left.
    There will be a white number on the right of "Today" that reminds you how many tasks should be done today.
  2. [Next], Here you can see what are the tasks you want to get to Next?
    To create a new "Next" task, click the plus icon on the bottom left.
    In oTask you can set a due date as well as a reminder for any task.
    After you have set up a due date, when a reminder is due, the related task will be listed in "Today" to call your attention.
  3. [Somday], Here you can see what do you want to do on someday?
    To create a new ‘Someday’ task, click the plus icon on the bottom left.
  4. [Done], Here you can see what have you done or cancelled?
    Even a task has been completed, you can change the stage to reopen it.
When creating a new task, you can put the task into any stage list you want.
You can also change the stage of an exist task to fix it's new priority or status.

How do I check the team tasks?

In oTask, you can see all the team tasks in four lists: Messages, Projects, People, Stages.

  1. [Messages], Here you can see all messages received on your tasks and tasks you are following.
    When there is a new activity on a shared task, such as someone modified the description, changed the status or assignee, you'll get a new message for it.
    Once a comment has been added, you'll also get a new message to let you know who has leaved feedback, asked a question or added extra insight.
    There will be a white number on the right of "Messages" that displays how many new messages are waiting for you to read.
    Tap on the "Messages" button to open the task list, you can see a blue conversation icon below the task title if there is a new message for this task.
  2. [Projects], Here you can see all the projects you are available to view.
    You can add tasks in a project, or invite people in it.
    All the tasks in a project are visible to each member of the same project.
  3. [People], Here you can see all the people in your space.
    Click on the people to review tasks assigned to him/her.
  4. [Stages], Here you can see all the tasks in "Today", "Next", "Someday", "Done" or "Cancelled".
    Click on the stage, you can see all tha tasks in it.

How do I edit my tasks?

Tap on the task item that you want to edit to open the detail view, then tap any field to edit it.
Every activity will be saved with a timestamp and immediately available for any members of this project to review.

How do I mark a task as completed?

【iPhone、iPad & Android】
To mark a task as completed, tap on the checkbox to the left of its title.
You also can click stage field of a task detail, and change it to "Done".
You can see your all completed tasks by tapping on the "Done" button at the bottom of My Tasks.

How do I share a task with other oTask users?

Sharing tasks is a great way to get things done with the help of friends, and work colleagues.
You must create a new project, and set up its visibility, then add those with whom you would like to share by clicking "Add ..." button located on the buttom of exist member list.
If the people you want to add is not in the member list, you'd better to return to home page, and tap on the "People" button in the Team Tasks, click the plus icon on the buttom left to send an invitation.
Add a new task in this project, it will be visible to each member of this project.

What is the visibility of a project?

There are two visibility levels: All Employees, Project Members.
If you choose "All Employees", the project and all the tasks in this project are visible to every member in your space.
If you choose "Project Member", only members added to this project can see it and all the tasks in the project.

Note: If you did not assign any project when creating a new task, no one can see this task but you.

How do I re-assign a task?

Tap on the task item that you want to edit to open the detail view, then tap "Assigned to" to edit it.
With oTask you can re-assign an unlimited amount of tasks to your work colleagues.

How do I enter a due date for a task?

In oTask you can set a due date as well as a reminder for any task. After you have set up a due date, when a reminder is due, the related task will be listed in "Today" to call your attention.
To enter a due date, tap a task to enter the detail view and then click on "Due Date".

Comments and Conversations

Once there is any activity below happened on your task or a task you are following, system will send you a message immediately, then you can view the number of new messages on the right of "Messages" at home page.

  1. Someone has edited a task, it will be saved with a timestamp.
  2. Someone assigned a new task to you.
  3. Someone started a conversation, leaved feedback, asked a question or added extra insight.

Security

We adhere to the highest industry standards for security.

Bank-Level Security

All transmissions to and from SteedOS including sign-on are encrypted at 256-bit through SSL via HTTPS.

Sophisticated Data Encryption

SteedOS uses state-of-the-art technology and industry best practices for data encryption during transit to and from the SteedOS cloud, as well as while stored within Hall.

Physical Security

SteedOS data centers are housed in nondescript facilities, and critical facilities have extensive setback and military grade perimeter control berms as well as other natural boundary protection. Physical access is strictly controlled both at the perimeter and at building ingress points by professional security staff utilizing video surveillance, state of the art intrusion detection systems, and other electronic means.

We Protect Your Data

All data is written to multiple disks instantly, backed up hourly, and stored in multiple locations.
